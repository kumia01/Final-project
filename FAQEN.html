Frequently asked questions


How/where do I find vegan recipes?
- You can find vegan recipes by marking off the checkbox inside the app.


I am allergic to different types of food. How can I find a recipe that’s is suited for me?
- Inside the app you can mark off different types of allergens when searching for recipes.


Dou you have to pay anything to use the app?
- No. It is totally free to use the app.

I forgot my password. How can I get help?
- Open the app and try to login. If the password is wrong, you will gain access to a “forgot my password” button. Press this and follow the instructions on the screen.
