About Food Rescue


Our Goal
Our goal is to reduce the mount of food waste. Norwegians waste hundreds of tons of food each year. By using our app you will be able to save money, while you take a step towards a better future for our planet.


Why Food Rescue?
Food rescue is created by students, and not by big powerful food-companies. We have a genuine wish to make a change. Food Rescue focuses on sustainable development, and not maximizing profit.


UN Sustainable Development Goals
Food Rescue focuses on UNs 13. sustainable development goal: Climate Action. We will through sustainability take action towards the goal.
Read more

Inspiration
The team behind Food Rescue came up with the idea about a waste-minimizing app, because life as a student can result in economical challenges. OsloMet University makes their students aware of climate-change, and encourages them to focus on sustainable development.
