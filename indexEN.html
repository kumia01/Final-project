We need to take action

Agriculture stands for 24% of global gas emissions. Production of food is a large contributor to greenhouse gas emissions. Misuse of food disregards all the energy, water, land, packaging and transportation that goes into production and distribution. World Wildlife Fund measures the approximate number of food wasted to 1.3 billion tons. The amount that gets thrown away is enough to feed every undernourished person in the world today.

What can we do?

The amount of discarded food can be reduced by utilizing everything that is already in the kitchen. However, practice shows that it is not as simple as it sounds. It predicts a certain know-how to make something out of nothing. Our purpose is to make it easier on the average consumer to use up all the ingredients they have in the kitchen. If enough people join the movement, together we can make a significant change.

Introdusing our app!

In simple terms, our application provides a reverse recipe search service, where users find meals through ingredients and not by their names. In this, we wish our users would find inspiration to utilize everything they already have in the kitchen without throwing anything out.

To visualise, let’s say that the following vegetables are in your refrigerator since the last time you cooked. You have some carrots, half an onion, apples, potatoes, ginger and garlic. You have chili flakes, stock and some type of beans in your cupboard as well. With this you can make a hearty carrot soup that will fill you up. The ax|pp shows you other possible recipes where you lack only one or two ingredients to make. It is up to you to decide how crucial those ingredients are.

Not only that!

You do not need an account to freely use our services. We want as many users as possible to find new and exciting recipes without wasting good ingredients. Our main goal is an environmental one, we simply wish (to save the planet) for people to care more about the energy that goes into food production.
